# Workflow Overview

(Write a 1-page overview of the pipeline stages and how OT-2 vs Flex are used.)

Opentrons OT-2 is an older model and is much more limited than the Opentrons Flex. As a result although we use OT-2s in this workflow they could easily be replaced with a Flex and would likely do the job faster. However due to the high output of samples from this lab they are used to reduce time bottlenecks and overreliance on the Flex.
